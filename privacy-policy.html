Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2024

1. Data Collection and Use

Our app, Smart ID Photo, uses AI technology to generate ID photos and portrait photos from user-uploaded life photos. During this process, we collect and process the following data:

  • User-uploaded photos: Used solely for generating ID photos and portrait photos.
  • Generated photos: Directly returned to the user's device after completion.

2. Data Storage and Deletion

User-uploaded and generated photos are temporarily stored on Alibaba Cloud's Singapore data center (AES-256 encrypted) during processing and are automatically deleted from the server within 30 minutes after processing.

3. Facial Data Processing

We take your facial data security very seriously. Below are the details of facial data processing:

  • Types of data collected: Facial feature points (e.g., positions of facial features, contours), no biometric data is collected.
  • Purpose of data use: Solely for generating ID photos and portrait photos.
  • Data storage location: Data is encrypted and transmitted to the Coze platform (ISO 27001 certified) for processing.
  • Data retention period: Automatically deleted within 30 minutes after processing.
  • Data sharing: Not shared with any third parties, except as required by law.

4. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. To delete your data, please contact: 759090921@qq.com.
