Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2024

1. Data Collection & Usage

Our app Smart ID Photo uses AI technology to generate official ID photos and portraits from users' uploaded personal photos. We process:

  • Uploaded Photos: Solely for generating ID/portrait photos
  • Generated Photos: Directly returned to user devices

2. Data Storage & Deletion

All photos are temporarily stored in Alibaba Cloud Singapore Data Center (AES-256 encrypted) and automatically deleted from servers within 30 minutes after processing.

3. Facial Data Processing

  • Collected Data: Facial landmarks (no biometric data)
  • Processing Flow: Upload → Encrypted transfer to Coze Platform (ISO 27001 certified) → AI processing → Return results
  • Data Sharing: Never shared with third parties except legal requirements

1. 数据收集与使用

我们的 App 智能证件照 通过用户上传的生活照片，利用 AI 技术生成证件照和形象照。在此过程中，我们收集并处理以下数据：

  • 用户上传的照片：仅用于生成证件照和形象照
